## Long Description

**Scanner Pro** is the fastest, cleanest QR code and barcode scanner on Android — built with a premium dark-mode design and everything you need in one app.

### 📷 Instant Scanning
Point your camera at any QR code or barcode and get results in milliseconds. Scanner Pro supports all major formats:
- QR Code
- EAN-13 / EAN-8
- Code 128 / Code 39
- UPC-A / UPC-E
- ITF / PDF-417

Results open instantly with one-tap actions: **Copy**, **Open Link**, **Share**, and **Save to Favorites**.

### ✏️ Powerful QR Generator
Create professional QR codes for 9 different content types:
- 🌐 URL / Website
- 📝 Plain Text
- 📶 Wi-Fi Network (auto-connects on scan)
- 👤 Contact Card (vCard)
- 📞 Phone Number
- 📧 Email
- 💬 SMS
- 📍 GPS Location
- 📱 Social Media Profile

Fully customize every QR code:
- Choose foreground and background colors
- Pick from 4 style presets: Classic, Dark, Ocean, Sunset
- Upload your own logo to embed inside the QR code
- Set error correction level (L / M / Q / H)
- Download as **PNG** or **high-quality PDF**

### 📋 Smart History
Every scan is automatically saved. Search by keyword, filter by format, sort by newest, oldest, or A–Z. Delete individual items or clear all. Star any scan to save it to Favorites.

### ⭐ Favorites
Your most important scans and generated QR codes in one place. Instantly re-scan or regenerate saved items. Small inline previews make it easy to identify each one at a glance.

### 🌙 Dark Mode
Beautiful deep-navy dark theme designed for comfortable use in any lighting. Toggle to light mode any time from the header.

### 🔒 100% Private
Scanner Pro works entirely on your device. No account required. No data uploaded. Your scan history lives only in your local storage.
